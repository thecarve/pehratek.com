What We Do

Pehratek Products was created in 1979 as a design lab for specialty products in the areas of audiology and health care. The first product created was the REINFORCER VRA (Visual Reinforcement Apparatus) introduced in late 1980. Combining elements of design innovation and superb product performance, its popularity grew as an easy-to-use, reliable pediatric testing system used by audiologists and pediatric clinics for testing children’s hearing world-wide. Today, Pehratek Products offers a line of innovative medical/audiology products that offer sound solutions to communications and hearing assessment issues.
